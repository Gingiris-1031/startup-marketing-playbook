# 🌿 Startup Marketing on a $0 Budget

No money? Fine. Every tactic in this playbook costs exactly $0 — the currency is your time and creativity. Organic channels only:

- **SEO from scratch** — ranking without backlinks or domain authority
- **Community infiltration** — providing value in existing communities without getting flagged as a spammer
- **Platform-native social content** — posts that drive signups organically
- **Word-of-mouth engineering** — making the product itself naturally shareable

🇺🇸 The bootstrapper's marketing manual: organic-only, zero ad spend, compounding channels.
🇨🇳 零预算创业营销手册：白手起家做 SEO、在现有社区里提供价值而不是刷屏、平台原生的社媒内容、把产品本身做成传播源——每一招花费都是 $0。
🇯🇵 予算ゼロのスタートアップ・マーケティング。ゼロからのSEO、既存コミュニティでの価値提供、口コミ設計まで、すべて無料の施策のみ。
🇰🇷 예산 제로 스타트업 마케팅: 맨땅에서 시작하는 SEO, 기존 커뮤니티에서 가치 제공, 입소문 설계까지 — 모든 전술이 $0.
