# Startup Marketing on $0 Budget — Organic Channels Only

No money? No problem. Every tactic here costs $0 — just your time and creativity.

## SEO from Scratch

How to rank on Google without backlinks or domain authority:

- **Long-tail keyword targeting**: Find low-competition queries your audience actually searches for
- **Content depth over breadth**: One definitive guide beats ten shallow posts
- **Technical foundations**: Site speed, mobile-first, structured data from day one
- **Programmatic SEO**: Template-driven pages for scalable organic traffic
- **Internal linking architecture**: Build topical authority through smart link structure

## Community Infiltration

Provide genuine value in existing communities without spamming:

- **Identify watering holes**: Where does your ICP hang out? (Reddit, Discord, Slack, forums)
- **Value-first engagement**: Answer questions, share insights — never lead with your product
- **Build reputation over time**: Consistent helpful presence → natural curiosity about what you're building
- **Community-native content**: Match the tone, format, and expectations of each community
- **Strategic timing**: Share your product only when directly relevant to someone's problem

## Social Media Playbook

Platform-specific content strategies that drive signups organically:

- **X/Twitter**: Threads documenting your journey, tactical insights, engagement with peers
- **LinkedIn**: Longer-form thought leadership, industry analysis, contrarian takes
- **Reddit**: Genuine participation in niche subreddits, AMAs, case study posts
- **YouTube**: Tutorial content, product walkthroughs, industry explainers
- **TikTok/Reels**: Quick tips, before/after demos, behind-the-scenes building

## Word-of-Mouth Engineering

How to make your product naturally shareable:

- **Remarkable moments**: Design "wow" experiences users can't help but tell others about
- **Social proof hooks**: Public usage indicators, badges, shareable achievements
- **Referral triggers**: In-product moments where inviting others feels natural
- **Story-worthy outcomes**: Help users achieve results they'll brag about
- **Low friction sharing**: One-click share, pre-written copy, visual assets

## Guerrilla Tactics

Creative, scrappy approaches that punch above their weight:

- **Piggyback on trends**: Newsjacking, meme marketing, cultural moment riding
- **Competitive positioning**: "Alternative to X" pages, migration guides, comparison content
- **Partnership hacking**: Cross-promotions with complementary non-competing products
- **Launch stacking**: Combine Product Hunt + HN + Reddit + Twitter for compounding effects
- **PR without budget**: HARO responses, founder story pitches, data-driven press angles
